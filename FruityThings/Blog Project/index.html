Classification of fruits, Dec 7, 2022

Simple Fruits

Simple fruits are those formed from a flower with a monocarpous pistil.
Simple fruits develop from a single carpel or from a compound ovary.
Simple fruits are the result of the result of the ripening-to-fruit of a simple or compound ovary in a single flower with a single pistle

Examples;
  1. Banana
  2. lemon
  3. Mango
follow this link for more examples of simple fruits."

Compound Fruits

Compound also called multiple fruits are formed from an inflorescence or bunch whose fruitlets are fused together to make, jingle large fruits.
A multiple fruit is a cluster of several ripened ovaries produced by several flowers crowned on the same inflorescence.

Examples;
  1. Pineapple
  2. Rasberry
  3. strawberry
